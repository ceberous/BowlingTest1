Our girl Angie

My name is Apenayaas Angelic Charmaine , you can just call me Angie. I was born in Denmark close to Copenhagen in 2012. My father is German and my mother is Danish. So you can say I am quite an international girl. I am strong and very sweet. I really love to have those humans around and I refuse to eat my food until they cuddle me. It took me some time to train them, but after a while they understood. They say that my fur is white as snow. Well, my favorite past time is digging, you should see my fur then. I love to play with Izzy, she is the coolest. My boss thinks I am funny when I get excited. He says it looks as if I am step dancing with my front legs. I enjoy having puppies because I can play with them like I play with Izzy's puppie Frodo. Like all the other Landseers here, I have been certified by the German Landseer Club for breeding
